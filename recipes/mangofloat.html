Mango Float

Description

Mango Float is a popular Filipino dessert made with layers of ripe mangoes, cream, and graham crackers. It's a no-bake treat that's perfect for hot weather.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe mangoes, peeled and sliced
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 package (200g) graham crackers
  • 1/2 cup milk (for soaking the crackers)

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the heavy cream and sweetened condensed milk. Whip until soft peaks form.
  2. In a rectangular dish, layer the graham crackers at the bottom.
  3. Add a layer of the cream mixture, followed by a layer of sliced mangoes.
  4. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of the cream mixture on top.
  5. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or until set.
  6. Slice and serve chilled. Enjoy your Mango Float!
