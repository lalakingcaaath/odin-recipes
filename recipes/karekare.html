Kare-Kare Recipe

Description

Kare-Kare is a Filipino oxtail stew made with a rich peanut sauce, often served with bagoong (fermented shrimp paste) on the side.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs oxtail
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup rice flour
  • 1/4 cup bagoong (fermented shrimp paste)
  • 1 banana heart, sliced
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 1/2 lb string beans
  • 6 cups water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large pot, boil the oxtail in water until tender.
  2. Remove the oxtail and set aside. In the same pot, add the peanut butter and rice flour, stirring until smooth.
  3. Add the oxtail back to the pot, along with the bagoong, banana heart, eggplant, and string beans.
  4. Simmer until the vegetables are tender. Add water as needed to achieve desired consistency.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with steamed rice.
