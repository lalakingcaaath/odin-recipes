Sinigang na Baboy

Description

Sinigang na Baboy is a traditional Filipino sour soup made with pork, tamarind, and various vegetables. It's known for its savory and tangy flavor, making it a comforting dish, especially during rainy days.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly, cut into cubes
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 medium onion, quartered
  • 2 medium tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 cup tamarind pulp (or 1 packet of sinigang mix)
  • 2 cups water spinach (kangkong)
  • 1 cup radish (labanos), sliced
  • 2-3 green chili peppers
  • Fish sauce (patis) to taste

Steps

  1. In a large pot, combine the pork belly and water. Bring to a boil and skim off any impurities.
  2. Add the onion and tomatoes. Simmer until the pork is tender.
  3. Stir in the tamarind pulp (or sinigang mix) and let it dissolve.
  4. Add the radish and green chili peppers. Cook for a few minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Finally, add the water spinach and season with fish sauce to taste. Serve hot.
