Burnt Basque Cheesecake

Description

Burnt Basque Cheesecake is a rich and creamy dessert that has a unique burnt top, giving it a distinct flavor. It's made with simple ingredients and is surprisingly easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar together until smooth.
  3. Add the heavy cream, eggs, vanilla extract, flour, and salt. Mix until well combined.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased 9-inch springform pan.
  5. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until the top is deeply browned and the center is set.
  6. Allow the cheesecake to cool before removing it from the pan.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
